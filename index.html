Alden's non Professional Guide To YBA


Section 1. Opinions On Stands

There are 38 stands in the game that I'm aware of. In section 1, I will go over every stand and my professional opinion on them in alphabetical order.


AEROSMITH

oh boy, where do i begin. This stand only has 3 moves that inflict damage. Propeller Charger, Vola Barrage, and Little Boy. Propeller Charge is an averge move. dealing 34 damage, it's alright in terms of combat. it's a short thrust forward with the plane. Vola Barrage, oh man oh man, is my least favourite move in the game. You can't cancel it, it goes on for a stupid amount of time, you're stunned like a barrage while in it, and Aerosmith can move while using the move. It needs to be nerfed damage wise, or reduce the time because as of right now, it does 1.3 damage every bullet for 9 seconds. as for Little Boy, it's an average move. it can't be blocked/parried, but you can at least cancel it. it drops 3 bombs, each of which doing 18 damage. Aerosmith's last move is Pilot, which doesn't do damage. but this move is also stupid broken. the Pilot goes really, really far. i'm not sure the xact distanc, but it's enough to spend a lot of time finding the user.
